Programming Enthusiast

As a passionate programmer, I thrive on solving complex problems and crafting efficient solutions. With expertise in C, C++, Python, and more, I've tackled challenges ranging from web development to machine learning. From internships at leading tech firms to personal projects like ReelRecs and FinFlow, I've honed my skills and embraced the ever-evolving landscape of technology.

Creative Visionary

Beyond the lines of code, I am a filmmaker and photographer, weaving stories through visuals and capturing moments that resonate. From directing short films to orchestrating photography events, I've explored the art of storytelling and visual expression. From modeling IoT-enabled home safety systems to developing robust software solutions like EmployWise and RealEstate Navigator, I've embraced interdisciplinary challenges with enthusiasm. Whether it's optimizing performance or crafting user-friendly applications, I'm committed to pushing boundaries and making a meaningful impact in the world of technology.
